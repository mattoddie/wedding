The Venue

We chose Rowhill Grange in Wilmington for our wedding. If you are wondering where that is, it's near Dartford, Kent (the place with a big bridge). We are crossing our fingers for a nice sunny day so the ceremony can take place outside in their gorgeous grounds.

If you would like to see more information about the venue, please visit the Rowhill Grange website. Accommodation is available at the venue for those that would like to stay there.

How to get there

NOTE: When using satellite Navigation to find Rowhill Grange, please use postcode BR8 7SQ

Arriving by road from M25 Clockwise

(Approx 5mins), turn off at Junction 3. Please note that there are 2 exits for junction 3, do not take the first exit marked M20, you require the 2nd exit for the A20 to London. Follow signs for Swanley on the B2173. At Swanley, follow signs for Hextable B258 through four small roundabouts. One and a half miles from the last roundabout you will see the entrance for Rowhill Grange on the left hand side opposite a red MOT Garage on the right.

By road from A2 London

Turn off at the Dartford Heath exit, signposted Foots Cray/Bexley/Wilmington and turn right at the roundabout. At mini roundabout, turn left towards Wilmington. Continue through a further two roundabouts taking the first turning on the right for Wilmington. Proceed to the top of this road. Turn left into Broad Lane. At the next roundabout turn right into Barn End Lane signposted Hextable and Swanley, which leads to Top Dartford Road (B258). Rowhill Grange is situated about 1 mile on the right hand side opposite a red MOT Garage on the left.

By rail

Trains run regularly from London Victoria to Swanley via Bromley South. Swanley station is about 5 minutes from the venue. If you would rather travel to Dartford, the station is about 10-15 minutes away.

Local taxis

We will provide a list of local taxis from the stations to the venue.
